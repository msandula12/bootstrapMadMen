Some general things about me.

Coding is a gateway drug to joining a bootcamp to become a web developer.

My name is Michael Sandula. I'm 27 years old. I graduated from Oakland University in 2010 with a bachelor's degree in journalism. Since then, I've worked as a copy editor for a few places, most recently The Oakland Press. But a year or so ago I started getting into coding, which as we all know is a gateway drug to wanting to join a bootcamp to become a web developer. And so here I am, a soon-to-be student of Grand Circus (Don't know where they are? See map below!).
